Add your country's flag to your Terraria world using World Country Flags!
Adds 129 new placeable flags, most of which are craftable with 10 silk at a new crafting station - the Globe (crafted at a Sawmill using 12 wood and 4 iron/lead bars).
Also adds 11 historical flags that are craftable with 10 silk and 1 gem.
